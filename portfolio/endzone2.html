My work

I worked at Gentlymad Studios as an intern and later as a working student for a total of one year. During this time, I worked (amongst other things) on the following tasks:

Prototype Expedition System

The expedition prototype was created during the pre-production phase and demonstrates the expedition gameplay, in which players explore a point of interest. In contrast to Endzone 1, the aim was to create a highly interactive gameplay in which the player directly controlls settlers.

Look Development: Overgrowth

A visual prototype for procedural overgrowth that reacts to environmental changes.

Look Development: Building Destruction

A visual prototype for dynamic procedural destruction.

Zone Preview Tool

I was involved in the creation of a tool that helps game designers to create "zones" (areas that can be settled). Game designers can paint areas of mountains, for example, and see the changes almost instantly in the zone preview.
My part was to create a shader that renders the preview of a zone.
