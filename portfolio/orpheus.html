My work

Developing the look and feel of the sound waves was my main task. I wrote shaders for said waves, and learned a lot about 3D rendering and real-time lighting in the process.
Also, I created an emission shader which was used to create the lyre and the glowing mushrooms (see images below).

Additionally, I worked closely with the game design and art departments to achieve smooth and responsive player controls.

About Orpheus

Orpheus is a game that was developed over the course of one semester as a project at my university Hochschule der Medien. The team consisted of 21 students from different courses of study and universities.

The game is set in a dark cave, but there's a twist: every sound produces light.
Players must enter the dark caverns of Hades' realm and use sounds to find a way through the levels, avoiding enemies along the way.
