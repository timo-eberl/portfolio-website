About Adverse

Adverse was developed over the course of one semester as a project at my university Hochschule der Medien. The team consisted of 22 students from different courses of study and universities.

The game is a fast-paced semi-open world game with stealth elements. It takes place in a futuristic neon punk city. The player's objective is to destroy and replace advertising posters using various abilities that can be unlocked throughout the game.
