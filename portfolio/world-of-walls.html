About World of Walls

World of Walls is a game prototype that was developed over one semester in a team of three at my university Hochschule der Medien as part of the lecture Gameplay Programming.

We have built walls with various functions to get a grasp of the architecture and functionality of Unreal Engine 5.
For example, I used Unreal Engine's Chaos System to create satisfying destruction.
